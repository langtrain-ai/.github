## What is Langtrain?

**Langtrain** is a privacy-first AI platform that brings powerful LLM fine-tuning directly to your machine. No cloud uploads. No data sharing. Complete control over your AI models.

We turn complex fine-tuning workflows into intuitive, elegant experiences—built for developers, researchers, and enterprises who value data sovereignty.

---

## Products

### Langtrain Studio
**Desktop application for offline LLM fine-tuning**

Fine-tune state-of-the-art language models entirely on your local machine with GPU acceleration.

- **100% Offline** — Your data never leaves your machine
- **GPU Optimized** — NVIDIA CUDA & Apple Silicon support
- **Advanced Training** — SFT, RLHF, DPO, LoRA, QLoRA
- **Real-time Monitoring** — Track training progress with live metrics
- **No-Code Interface** — Intuitive UI for all skill levels

**Supported Models:** Llama 3, Mistral, Qwen, DeepSeek, Phi, Gemma, and more.

---

### Langtrain Hub
**Cloud platform for model management & deployment**

Centralized hub for managing fine-tuned models, datasets, and deployments.

- **Model Registry** — Version and organize your fine-tuned models
- **Dataset Management** — Upload, transform, and prepare training data
- **One-Click Deploy** — Deploy models to production endpoints
- **Team Collaboration** — Share models and datasets across your organization
- **Usage Analytics** — Monitor API usage and model performance

## Training Methods

- **SFT** (Supervised Fine-Tuning) — Train on instruction-response pairs
- **RLHF** (Reinforcement Learning from Human Feedback) — Align models with human preferences
- **DPO** (Direct Preference Optimization) — Efficient preference learning
- **LoRA** — Low-Rank Adaptation for efficient fine-tuning
- **QLoRA** — Quantized LoRA for consumer GPUs
